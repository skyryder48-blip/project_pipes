## Unique Weapon Mechanics

### DA/SA Trigger (P226, MK25, P220, P229, PX4)
- First shot slightly slower/less accurate (DA)
- Subsequent shots improved (SA mode)

### SAO Target Trigger (P210)
- Consistent ultra-light pull (3.5 lbs)
- Best precision at cost of capacity

### Striker-Fired (P320, Dagger, SR9)
- Consistent trigger pull
- No hammer to catch on clothing

### Rotating Barrel (PX4 Storm)
- 30-45° rotation distributes recoil
- Significantly reduced felt recoil
- **NO SUPPRESSOR SUPPORT**

### AR Platform (UDP-9, Blue ARP)
- Extended effective range
- Higher velocity from longer barrel
- Different ergonomics/handling

## Installation

1. Add stream assets to each weapon's `/stream/` folder
2. Add to server.cfg:
```cfg
# Ammunition resources (ensure these load first)
ensure ammo_9mm
ensure ammo_45acp

# Batch 11 Weapons
ensure weapon_sigp226
ensure weapon_sigp226mk25
ensure weapon_sigp210
ensure weapon_sigp220
ensure weapon_sigp229
ensure weapon_sigp320
ensure weapon_px4storm
ensure weapon_psadagger
ensure weapon_rugersr9
ensure weapon_udp9
ensure weapon_bluearp
```

3. Register with ox_inventory

## Balance Philosophy

Batch 11 offers nuanced choices across the 9mm spectrum:

**Precision Players:** P210 Target (best accuracy, limited capacity)
**Military/Duty:** P226 MK25, P320 (proven combat platforms)
**Budget Conscious:** PSA Dagger (good enough at half the price)
**PDW Role:** UDP-9 (extended range, AR ergonomics)
**Recoil Sensitive:** PX4 Storm (rotating barrel advantage)
**Classic Fans:** P226, P220 (DA/SA heritage)
